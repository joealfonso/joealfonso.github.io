Adam Levine Shop Your Way Application


Screens from the Adam Levine application within the Shop Your Way application. This was our groups first major piece of mobile work from start to finish and was by far our best learning experience. The scope included everything from branding to functional prototypes handed off to engineering.

My responsibilities were to work with the other designer to make sure all graphic elements followed Apple's Human Interface Guidelines and Android's design standards while maintaining a consistant brand standard and visual look that would translate well across both mobile operating systems. This involved multiple style guide iterations, reading countless documentation guides and books on mobile design, and most of all, the Pixel Perfect Precision handbook PDF(in my opinion, the Holy Grail of information for interaction and digital design).
