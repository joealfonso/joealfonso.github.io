Aurasma Humor Animation

A fun motion graphics piece I did for our alternative reality application for our t-shirt humor campaign. I was able to do almost whatever I wanted with the style of my choice, as long as it communicated the message clearly. I decided to have some fun with inertia expressions and create something fun that would reward the user for holding up their phone when activating the animation.
