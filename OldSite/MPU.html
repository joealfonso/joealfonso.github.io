Merchandise Pickup Mobile Application


A mobile project I did that originally started as a redesign for the Merchandise Pick Up application for Sears that saw a few progressive enhancements including a total rebranding to the Shop Your Way brand. The redesign included a whole new look as well as different button and form field states as well. One of the goals I accomplished was to really bring this application with modern mobile standards and guidelines while also introducing the business units to some new patterns that could make the application not only more functional but also enjoyable to use.
